Survivor’s Manifesto

You don’t have to heal. You don’t have to forgive. You don’t have to be anyone’s idea of “inspirational.”

You can be angry, broken, messy, loud, or silent. You can rage. You can cry. Or you can laugh in the face of the universe and dare it to try again.

Survival isn’t pretty. It’s not linear, and it sure as hell isn’t polite. If you’re still here—if you’re still breathing—then you’ve already won a battle most people will never understand.

Permission granted to live, love, grieve, and raise hell on your own terms.

No platitudes. No apologies. No surrender.

— Cassandra Crossno

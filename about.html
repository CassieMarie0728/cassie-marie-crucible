About the Broad Who Wrote This Shit

I’m Cassandra Crossno. Navy vet, widow, professional survivor, and the only “grief author” who’ll tell you the truth even if it makes you cry or want to punch a wall (or both). My life? Let’s just say the universe ran out of lemons and started hurling bricks.

I lost Patrick—my best friend, my love, my anchor. In the aftermath, my family took “betrayal” to new levels, and I spent six months homeless, scraping by on rage and spite. That’s how this voice got forged—somewhere between heartbreak, righteous fury, and the kind of bone-deep resilience you only get when you’ve been through hell and refused to fuckin' stay.

Why do I write like this? Because the world doesn’t need more sugary-coated horseshit. It needs the ugly, brutal, honest truth. You’ll find it here—scars, F-bombs, and all.

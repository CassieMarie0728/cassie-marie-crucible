FAQ (Freakin’ Annoying Questions)

The dumb, offensive, or exhausted questions I get all the time—answered with the snark they deserve.

Q: “Aren’t you worried your honesty offends people?”
A: Only the ones who deserve it.
Q: “Why do you swear so much?”
A: Because “golly gee whiz, my heart is broken” doesn’t cut it.
Q: “Will you ever write a ‘healing’ book?”
A: If the definition of healing is “telling the truth and surviving another day,” then I already did. Otherwise, no.
Q: “Can I recommend you to my church group?”
A: Only if you want to get banned. (Worth it.)
Q: “Do you ever run out of rage?”
A: Not in this lifetime, cupcake.

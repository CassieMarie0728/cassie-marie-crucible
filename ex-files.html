The Ex-Files

A tongue-in-cheek gallery for the ghosts, the backstabbers, and the ones who “just couldn’t handle your energy.” No names, just vibes and a little cathartic shade.

“The Ghoster”

Vanished right after the funeral. Left behind a casserole and nothing else.

“The Fixer”

Had a solution for everything except their own inability to listen.

“The Inspirational Mug Giver”

Gave me a mug that said ‘Live, Laugh, Love.’ I use it to water my cactus now.

“The Family Betrayer”

Tried to rewrite my story for their own comfort. I kept the receipts.
